Our Roastery
Our Story

Coffee is a quiet craft. We treat it that way.

Drift began with a simple idea: slow down. In a world of instant everything, we wanted to create a space where coffee was something you lingered over — not rushed through.

We source beans from farms we've visited in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala. Every batch is roasted on-site in our Petone roastery, profiled to bring out the character of each origin. No shortcuts, no filler blends.

The cafe on Jackson Street is an extension of that philosophy — warm light, unhurried mornings, and a cup worth sitting down for.

What We Believe

Three principles, every roast

01

Source with transparency

Every bag tells you who grew it, where, and at what altitude. We pay above Fair Trade minimums because good coffee starts with fair relationships.

02

Roast with restraint

We roast to highlight, not to mask. Light to medium profiles that let the bean speak — stone fruit from Ethiopia, chocolate from Colombia, citrus from Guatemala.

03

Serve with warmth

Whether you're a first-time flat white drinker or a home-brewing obsessive, you belong here. Coffee should be welcoming, not intimidating.
