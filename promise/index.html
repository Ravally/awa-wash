Clean Home.
Greener Aotearoa.

With every wash, we fund native tree planting across New Zealand. It's not a gimmick — it's a commitment to leaving our country better than we found it. Naturally clean homes, naturally regenerating forests.

What Sets Us Apart

Owner-Operated. Every Job.

Kurt personally oversees every single job. You're not getting a random subcontractor who doesn't know your name — you're getting the owner of the business, the bloke who staked his reputation on doing things right. That's a level of accountability you won't find with the big chains.

Honest Pricing. No Surprises.

The quote we give you is the price you pay. We don't tack on hidden fees, upsell services you don't need, or conveniently "discover" extra work once we're on-site. Transparent pricing, always. All prices are clearly listed on our website so you know what to expect before you even call.

Wellington Born. Kiwi Owned.

We're not a franchise. We're not part of a chain. We're a local Wellington family business that supports NZ-owned suppliers and puts money back into the community. When you hire Awa Wash, you're supporting your neighbours.
