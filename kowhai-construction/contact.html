Common Questions

How quickly can you provide a quote?

For most commercial projects, we can provide an indicative estimate within 5 business days of receiving your brief. Detailed fixed-price quotes typically take 2-3 weeks depending on project complexity.

Do you work outside Christchurch?

Yes — while Christchurch is our home base, we regularly deliver projects across the South Island including Queenstown, Dunedin, Timaru, and Ashburton.

What size projects do you take on?

We work across a wide range — from $500K fitouts through to $50M+ commercial developments. Every project gets the same level of attention and professionalism.

Can you work on occupied buildings?

Absolutely. Our fitout team specialises in staged construction within occupied buildings, minimising disruption to your business operations.
