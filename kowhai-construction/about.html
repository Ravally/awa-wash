About Us

Built on Canterbury Soil. Driven by Canterbury Pride.

From our roots in 1999 to leading the rebuild after the earthquakes, Kōwhai Construction has grown into one of the South Island's most trusted commercial builders—because we believe in doing things right.

Our Story

Founded in 1999 by Rawiri Henare, Kōwhai Construction started with a simple belief: that building should be done with integrity, craftsmanship, and a genuine commitment to our community. What began as a small team of five has grown into one of Canterbury's leading commercial builders, known for reliability, quality, and transparency.

When the 2011 Canterbury earthquakes struck, Kōwhai Construction wasn't just a construction company—we became part of the recovery. We led major rebuild projects across the region, earning the trust of businesses and communities who were rebuilding their futures. That experience shaped who we are: resilient, community-focused, and driven by a responsibility that goes beyond contracts and timelines.

Our Journey

1999
Founded by Rawiri Henare with a team of 5. Started taking on local commercial projects in Christchurch with a commitment to quality and integrity.
2004
Completed our first $10M+ commercial project. This milestone marked our transition from regional builder to major commercial contractor.
2011
Canterbury earthquake response & rebuild begins. We became integral to the region's recovery, managing over 40 major rebuild projects.
2015
Expanded to Queenstown and Dunedin. Growing our footprint across the South Island while maintaining our commitment to local relationships.
2018
ISO 45001 certification achieved. Formal recognition of our industry-leading safety standards and practices.
2022
$100M annual revenue milestone. A testament to our team's dedication and the trust our clients place in us.
2025
340+ projects completed. From small commercial fits to major multi-million dollar builds, we've earned our reputation one project at a time.
