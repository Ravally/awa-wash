Little Fern Early Learning is a boutique early childhood centre in the heart of Wellington. Founded in 2019, we believe children learn best when they're free to explore, question, and get their hands dirty.

Our curriculum blends Te Whāriki — New Zealand's early childhood framework — with nature-based pedagogy, giving tamariki the space to develop at their own pace in an environment that feels like a second home.
