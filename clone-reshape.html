Our Space

A warehouse gym built different.

Neon Fitness 24/7 is housed in a converted industrial warehouse in the heart of Lower Hutt. Raw concrete, exposed steel, and neon-lit training zones — designed to push you beyond comfortable.

Open around the clock, seven days a week. Whether you're an early riser, a lunch-break warrior, or a midnight grinder — the doors are always open and the lights are always on.
