Guided adventures in the heart of Queenstown

Discover Tūī Trail Co.: guided hiking and adventure tours through Queenstown's most breathtaking landscapes. From alpine ridgelines to ancient beech forest, every trail is led by local guides who know these mountains like home. Small groups, big views, and a deep respect for the land beneath your boots.

Our trails
Full day Alpine
Ben Lomond From $189

A legendary summit hike with panoramic views across Lake Wakatipu and the Remarkables. Ascend through native bush into the alpine zone for a full day of stunning ridgeline walking.

Half day Lakeshore
Bob's Cove From $95

A peaceful lakeside walk through ancient rata and beech forest. Perfect for photography lovers, with hidden beaches and views of the Remarkables reflected in still water.

2 days Overnight
Routeburn Track From $495

Two days on one of New Zealand's Great Walks. Cross mountain passes, traverse hanging valleys, and sleep in a backcountry hut beneath the stars. An unforgettable overnight adventure.

Full day River valley
Moonlight Track From $165

Descend through old gold-mining country along the Shotover River. Rocky gorges, swing bridges, and wide-open tussock basins — a trail rich with history and wild beauty.

Half day Sunrise
Queenstown Hill From $85

Catch the first light breaking over the Remarkables on our early-morning guided climb. A short but rewarding ascent with 360-degree views and fresh coffee at the summit.

3 days Multi-day
Greenstone Caples From $695

A three-day loop through two stunning valleys. Wide river flats, mossy beech forest, and a mountain pass connecting the Greenstone and Caples valleys — classic backcountry tramping.

Our Regions

Queenstown Basin

Queenstown sits in a dramatic basin carved by ancient glaciers, ringed by the Remarkables and framed by Lake Wakatipu. Our day hikes depart from the town centre and climb into alpine country within minutes. Crystal-clear rivers, dense beech forest, and gold-rush history at every turn.

Glenorchy & Beyond

Forty-five minutes from Queenstown along one of the world's most scenic drives, Glenorchy is the gateway to Mt Aspiring National Park and the start of New Zealand's Great Walks. Wide braided rivers, towering peaks, and landscapes so pristine they've doubled for Middle-earth.

Safety & Sustainability

Every Tūī Trail guide holds wilderness first aid certification and carries a PLB. We operate under DOC concession, follow Leave No Trace principles, and offset every trip's carbon footprint. Your safety and the health of these mountains are never compromised — so you can focus on the walk ahead.
