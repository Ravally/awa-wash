Built on faith, family, and a pressure washer.

"I just want to do honest work for good people."

Awa Wash started the way most good things do — with a bloke, a trailer, and a determination to do things properly. Kurt grew up on the Kapiti Coast, where the salt air hits everything and the houses need a good scrub more often than most. He saw an industry full of no-shows, half-jobs, and people who didn't care. So he picked up a pressure washer and decided to be different.

A proud husband and father, Kurt brings the same care to every job that he brings to his family. His faith grounds everything he does — show up when you say you will, treat people's homes like your own, leave the place better than you found it. No shortcuts. No excuses. Just hard work done with integrity.

From the Kapiti Coast to your doorstep

Growing up on the Kapiti Coast gave Kurt an appreciation for the outdoors that's never left. The hills, the coastline, the bush — it's all part of why he does what he does. Keeping homes clean isn't just a job, it's about preserving the places where families make their memories.

"When I finish a job and the homeowner comes outside and just goes 'wow' — that's it. That's the whole reason I do this."

What started as a one-man operation with a pressure washer and a ute has grown into a trusted Wellington-area service that homeowners recommend to their neighbours. But the core hasn't changed — Kurt still personally oversees every job, still answers the phone, and still believes a handshake means something.
