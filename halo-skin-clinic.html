Our Philosophy

Where science meets skin artistry

Founded by Dr. Lina Chen, Halo Skin Clinic was born from a belief that exceptional skincare should feel as good as it looks. Our Auckland CBD studio is a sanctuary — a place where clinical expertise meets calm, considered care.

Every treatment plan is crafted after a thorough skin analysis, because no two faces tell the same story. We listen first, then elevate.

Medical Grade

Only clinically proven devices and pharmaceutical-grade products enter our treatment rooms.

Bespoke Plans

No cookie-cutter protocols. Your treatment plan is uniquely yours, evolving with your skin.

Gentle Approach

We believe in subtle, cumulative results — never the overdone look. Natural radiance, always.

Ongoing Care

Your journey doesn't end at the door. Follow-up guidance and aftercare come standard.
