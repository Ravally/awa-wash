Who We Are

Christchurch-Built.
Canterbury-Proven.

Founded in 1999, Kōwhai Construction has grown from a small team of dedicated builders into one of Canterbury's most trusted commercial construction firms. Through the rebuild and beyond, we've been shaping the skyline of the South Island.

Our philosophy is simple: build it right, build it once, and build relationships that last longer than concrete. Every project benefits from our deep local knowledge, proven systems, and a team that genuinely cares about the outcome.

What We Do

Full-Scope Commercial
Construction

From ground-up builds to seismic strengthening, our team delivers across every phase and scale of commercial construction.

Commercial New Builds

Office towers, retail complexes, and industrial facilities built from the ground up. We manage every stage from foundation to fit-out.

Seismic Strengthening

Post-earthquake expertise you can trust. We've strengthened over 80 commercial buildings across Canterbury to meet current NBS requirements.

Fitout & Refurbishment

Transform existing spaces with minimal disruption. Our fitout team specialises in occupied building works for retail, hospitality, and office environments.

Project Management

End-to-end project management with real-time reporting, transparent budgeting, and a single point of contact from pre-construction through handover.

Sustainable Building

Green Star and Homestar-rated construction. We integrate sustainable materials, energy-efficient systems, and waste reduction into every build.

Design & Build

A single contract, one team, complete accountability. Our design-build approach streamlines delivery and keeps your vision intact from start to finish.
